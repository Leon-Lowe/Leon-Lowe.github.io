About

Hi there! My name is Leon Lowe and I'm a programmer from Sheffield, UK. My skillset mainly lies in Unity and C#, however I have also experimented with Python, Unreal, C++, and HTML. I use Unity and C# every day as I'm always looking to learn new things in the engine. I'm also eager to learn more about the other languages and engines I have expermented with, as well as learn new languages such as C and JavaScript.

As for my hobbies, I enjoy relaxing with my family (that could be watching a movie with them or going to the park wth my daughter), playing games wth my friends, and creating games. While playing games, I often find myself wondering how a dev team has created a certain mechanic and then trying to recreate that mechanic in Unity. These machnics could be something simple like an inventory, or something a little more complex like setting player hand positions dynamically with inverse kinematics.
